Data Dashboard

Overview
In working with the hypothetical client, Adidas, our team created a data-driven dashboard centered around customer experience to address the needs of three different personas. Our solution was the Adidashboard that incorporated data on web analytics, competitor sales, customer reviews, and social media trends based on geographic region.

Project Details
Context: Interaction Design Studio 1, CMU
Team: Shai Bhardwaj, Eric Li, Zach Van Cleef, Janelle Wen
Timeline: October-November, 2020
