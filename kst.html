Kelly Strayhorn Theater

Overview
My team worked to create a virtual streaming platform the Kelly Strayhorn Theater can use to conduct virtual classes, thereby increasing engagement with both audience and artists as well as generating revenue.

Project Details
Client: Kelly Strayhorn Theater
Context: User-Centered Research and Evaluation Course, CMU
Team: Janie Xue, Piper Hayden, Brent Hong
Role: Design Lead
Timeline: September-December, 2020

Problem
Kelly Strayhorn Theater, located in the East Liberty neighborhood of Pittsburgh, partnered with our User-Centered Research and Evaluation class to increase audience engagement, donations, and attendance at virtual events.

Solution
Our solution was a virtual streaming platform that KST could use to facilitate online events, including dance classes and community dialogues.

My Role
In addition to working to recruit and interview participants, I worked with my team to generate insights that would create value both for Kelly Strayhorn Theater and its patrons. I served as the Design Lead on our project, and was responsible for generating ideas for solutions that addressed user needs as well as guiding the prototyping process.
